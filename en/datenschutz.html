2. Collection and storage of personal data

When you access our website, your browser automatically sends information to our server. This includes, among other things:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referrer URL

This data is collected solely to ensure the smooth operation of the website and to evaluate system security and stability. It is not intended to be linked to any specific person. The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest).

3. Contact

If you contact us by email or phone, the data you provide (name, contact details, content of your enquiry) will be processed and stored for the purpose of handling your request. The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (contract initiation) or Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest). The data will be deleted after your request has been dealt with, unless statutory retention obligations apply.

4. Integration of external services (configurator)

Our website integrates an external configurator. When using the configurator, data may be transmitted to the respective provider. Please refer to the configurator provider’s privacy policy. We have concluded a data processing agreement with the provider in accordance with Art. 28 GDPR where required.

5. Cookies

This website may use technically necessary cookies that are required for the operation of the site (e.g. session cookies). The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR. You can configure your browser to notify you when cookies are set and to allow or block them individually.

6. Your rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
  • Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR)
  • Right to withdraw consent (Art. 7(3) GDPR)

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority (Art. 77 GDPR).

7. Changes

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy to reflect changes in the law or our services.
