How a project with 123 Terrassendach works

From the first consultation to installation, we guide you step by step – transparent, reliable and with clearly calculable prices.

1

Consultation & configuration

Configure your canopy online or get advice by phone. Together we clarify dimensions, orientation and desired equipment.

2

Planning, statics & production

After placing your order, we carry out the technical check, structural calculation and production in Germany – including powder coating in your desired colour.

3

Delivery & installation

The canopy is delivered directly from the factory – optionally including installation by our partner network or for convenient self-installation with detailed instructions.
